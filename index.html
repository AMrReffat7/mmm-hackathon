ABOUT US

Inspired by Rick and Morty's adventures through different dimensions, we dare you to explore a world where sophisticated AI, robotics, and Arduino technology are commonplace. In order to overcome the obstacles in this realm, you must be creative and push the limits of these advanced technologies. Come and participate in a day filled with creative pursuits, teamwork, and technological innovations as we delve into the worlds of artificial intelligence and robotics, addressing difficult challenges and creating innovative solutions for the future.

HackClub

Hack Club is a global non-profit network of high school coding clubs where members learn to code through building projects. Founded in 2014, the organization aims to empower students by providing resources, a supportive community, and mentorship to create their own tech projects and solve real-world problems. Hack Club fosters a hands-on, self-directed learning environment where creativity and collaboration are encouraged. Through workshops, hackathons, and online support, Hack Club helps students gain practical coding skills, develop their problem-solving abilities, and connect with a network of like-minded peers passionate about technology and innovation.
